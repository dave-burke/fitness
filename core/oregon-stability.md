# Oregon Project Stability Routine

1. Hot Salsa (5)
2. Side Plank, Knee to Chest (12)
3. Reverse Clamshell (2x20)
4. Clamshell (2x20)
5. Stability Ball Mountain Climbers (30)
6. Runner Touch (2x10)
7. Jane Fonda (2x20)
8. (Optional)Runner Pulls (15)
